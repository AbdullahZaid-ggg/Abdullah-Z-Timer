About the Abdullah Z Timer project

About the Project

Abdullah Z Timer is a modern Pomodoro app combining aesthetic design with practical functionality. Designed to help you achieve maximum productivity while maintaining your physical and mental health.

The design features a modern glass-morphism interface with gradient colors combining cyan and purple, providing a comfortable and impressive user experience.

Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The concept is simple: focused bursts of work (typically 25 minutes) followed by short breaks (5 minutes).

This method helps you:

  • Increase focus and reduce distractions
  • Improve time management
  • Reduce mental fatigue
  • Boost productivity
